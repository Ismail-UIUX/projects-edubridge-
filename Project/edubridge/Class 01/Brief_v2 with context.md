Project: CRM Dashboard Experience Design

Project Brief - With Context

You are a senior UX strategist and SaaS product designer.

Context for every output:

Users are from Bangladesh and primarily work in sales and customer support operations.
Most users access the CRM from low to mid-range Android devices with inconsistent internet connectivity.
Many users are not highly tech-savvy and prefer simple, familiar interaction patterns.
Teams work in fast operational environments where quick access to leads, follow-ups, and customer history is more important than advanced visual dashboards.
Users frequently mix Bangla and English while navigating products and entering customer information.
Heavy animations, large data loads, and overly complex navigation reduce usability on common devices.
Managers mainly care about quick visibility into team activity, pending follow-ups, and customer response status.
Sales representatives prioritize speed, reminders, and easy lead updates while working on the move.
Trust, clarity, and operational efficiency are more important than modern visual aesthetics.
Notification overload is a common issue, so prioritization and clarity in alerts are important.
Most users are already familiar with simple interaction patterns similar to bKash and local mobile applications.

Project Brief:

Design a mobile-first CRM platform for sales teams and customer support managers.

The platform should help users:

manage leads
track customer interactions
monitor sales pipelines
assign follow-up tasks
view customer activity history
manage support conversations
receive real-time notifications

The goal is to improve team productivity, reduce missed follow-ups, and simplify customer management workflows.

Target users:

Sales representatives
Customer support agents
Team managers

Key requirements:

Clean and easy-to-understand dashboard
Fast navigation between customer records
Simple task management flow
Clear customer activity timeline
Mobile usability considerations
Notification and reminder system
Lightweight and scalable UI system

Please provide:

UX strategy
user flow recommendations
dashboard structure
feature prioritization
mobile usability suggestions
onboarding recommendations
UI/UX improvement opportunities
